Frequently Asked Questions

Why are tiny homes on wheels?

Tiny homes on wheels are an economical way to live and travel. Instead of investing in a permanent residence, you can build or purchase tiny home that’s designed for mobility. This means that you can take your house with you if you move, or even explore the country without having to stay in hotels.

How long do tiny homes last?

Tiny homes are generally quite durable and can last for many years with proper maintenance. The lifespan of a tiny home is largely dependent on the materials used to build it, as well as how often it’s maintained. Generally speaking, tiny homes can last upwards of 20-30 years or more if they’re properly cared for.
